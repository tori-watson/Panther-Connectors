Mission Statement

Shop A College believes all Americans deserve to have the opportunity to attend college and enrich their lives with a rich education to support themselves in challenging careers. Without an education, students become stuck within a system that can sometimes become impossible to climb back up. Shop A College's goal is to create a nationally-recognized college fund for all students to access and for all able citizens to support them. With the help of thousands of donations from viewers, student debt, dropout rates, and great numbers of homeless can greatly fall on a decline.

Shop A College partners up with QVC, a free-to-air TV network shopping channel, to sponsor colleges in need through short ads rotating on the air for twenty-four hours. Viewers watching will see the thirty to forty-second long videos and have the chance to donate to the college of their choice. With a link and QR code at the end of every video, viewers will interact with Shop A College's website to shop for a college by being rewarded for their donations. Every dollar counts towards helping a student in need continue their education. There are always colleges and students in need that cannot access enough funding for students. We want to increase students' livelihoods to survive and set a soaring path for their future. With every donation, donors are funding projects that may have taken double times the amount of time it would have taken it without additional funding. Students will be able to lessen the pain of hundreds-thousands dollars of debt just to have a job. Help these students by being their light and supporting them for a better and brighter future.


Charitable gifts to SAC are tax deductible.
