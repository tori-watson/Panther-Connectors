Shop A College

Colleges students have always struggled to pay for their education. Financial aid has always been needed, but not everyone has a chance to earn funding. Through partnering with QVC, viewers will be able to quickly and accessibly donate to the college of choosing. Through 30 - 40 sec long ads, viewers will scan the code or search the link to our website sending them to donation site for that college. With your help, we can reduce the amount debt students gain through trying to bettr themselves in a society driven by work. To help those in need, please donate and spread the word!
